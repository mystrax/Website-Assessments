TEKKEN 7 TAKES FIGHTING GAMES TO NEW HEIGHTS

RETURN TO THE ROOTS OF TEKKEN

TEKKEN 7 is the latest instalment in the 27-year-old TEKKEN franchise and has
sold more than 7 million units worldwide since its release in June 2017. The
TEKKEN series has sold more than 51 million units over its entire lifetime.

TEKKEN 7 boasts millions of active players, an official eSportsWorld Tour, and a
wealth of new content for both new and old-school fans to enjoy, as well as
continued support through Season Passes and DLC characters!

Love, Revenge, Pride. We all have a reason to fight. Values are what define us
and make us human, regardless of our strengths or weaknesses. There are no
wrong motives, just the paths we choose to take.

Experience the epic showdown of the Mishima clan and unravel the reasons
behind each and every step of their ceaseless struggle. TEKKEN 7 features
stunning, story-driven cinematic battles and intense duels that can be enjoyed
with friends and rivals alike through innovative fight mechanics.
