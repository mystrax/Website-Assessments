About BANDAI NAMCO








Part of the Japanese BANDAI NAMCO Group, a leader in the global entertainment industry,
BANDAI NAMCO Entertainment Europe publishes and distributes video games
and entertainment products in Europe, Middle-East, Africa and Asia Pacific.
Our group’s mission is to provide “more fun for everyone” all around the world
with a goal to become the leading innovator in global entertainment.
Based out of our headquarters in Lyon (the culinary capital of France!)
we’re responsible for such titles as PAC-MAN, SOULCALIBUR, TEKKEN and DARK SOULS…
as well as new projects like LITTLE NIGHTMARES, TWIN MIRROR and THE DARK PICTURES.
