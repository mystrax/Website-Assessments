little nightmares

Immerse yourself in the unsettling world of Little Nightmares and help a tough young kid
known as Six tiptoe safely through the shadows of the Maw and escape to freedom.

But with many foul surprises waiting round every corner, Six's journey will be delectably
dangerous!

Do you remember your childhood fears?

Confront them in Little Nightmares, a dark whimsical tale where you’ll play as Six, the girl in the
yellow raincoat. Help her escape the Maw – a vast, mysterious vessel inhabited by corrupted
souls looking for their next meal.

little nightmares's lore

All Little Nightmares adventures take place in dark places, where the protagonists must
face their deepest fears and monstruous urges.

Embrace a child's vision distorted by scare: between dreams and nightmares, try to escape
hostile environments. Fortunately, you will sometimes find some help on these dangerous traps.

Grownups are the ones to fear. No need to discuss with them to know that they mean you
harm.

Each episode has a weak and heavy atmosphere, offering you a delectably dangerous journey
where you will have to go beyond your limits to escape.

Be brave, little ones.
